Belle is a smart and adventurous young woman who lives in a small village with her father, Maurice, an inventor.
When Maurice gets lost in the forest on his way to a fair, he accidentally discovers a mysterious castle and is captured by the Beast who lives there.
Hearing that her father is missing, Belle goes searching for him and finds the castle.
To save her father, she bravely offers to take his place as the Beast’s prisoner.
The Beast agrees, and Belle stays in the castle, beginning her journey in this strange and magical place.
