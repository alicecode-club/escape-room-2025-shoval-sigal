The Cursed Prince

Long ago, a young prince lived in a shining castle.
Though he had everything, he was selfish and unkind.
One winter night, a beggar woman offered him a rose for shelter.
He rejected her, and she revealed herself as a beautiful enchantress.
As punishment, she turned him into a beast and cast a spell on the castle.
The rose was enchanted and would bloom until his 21st year.
If he could love and be loved in return before the last petal fell, the spell would break.
If not, he would remain a beast forever.
